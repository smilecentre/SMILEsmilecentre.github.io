About Us

Who We Are

Recognising the regrettable chaotic social developments that abound in a culture of sex outside of marriage — and the happiness, peace, and social order that prevail in a sex-within-marriage culture — the name Sexual Morality Internship for Life Empowerment (SMILE) was founded.

SMILE brings together a growing network of over 50 voluntary workers from diverse communities and organisations, all united by one purpose — ensuring a moral culture change that is essential to prevent child abuse.

With collaborators gathered under the 3½-year Moral Culture Change to Prevent Child Abuse Campaign (which ended on 31 July 2022), SMILE successfully developed the national Sexual Reproductive Health #Best Interest of the Child (SRH #BIC) Policy Template — an alternative to the Sexual Reproductive Health Rights framework.

SMILE continues as the lead partner in implementing this policy through a 5-year SRH #BIC Movement (2023–2028), working collaboratively to build a Zimbabwe where children grow up in moral, loving, and safe communities.
